Find. Compare. Book. Done.

For parents, finding and booking camps for kids can be difficult and frustrating. This app changes that! No more Internet searching, hounding friends for recommendations, or getting camp information from the crumpled flyer in your child's backpack.

KidCampGuide is an online marketplace connecting camps with the people who want to use them. See all of the options, search based on your availability, register one time only, and book all of your camps in one place.

You can find camp options you may not have been aware of, you'll know when camps open up their registrations, and you'll know when they have last minute availability for when an unexpected business trip throws a wrench in your schedule. You'll also be able to connect to your social circles, so you'll know that Susie and Erica are doing the soccer camp next week, in case you want to sign up too.
